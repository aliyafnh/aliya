CHOOSING PEACE

The moment I stopped explaining

I used to explain myself until I was exhausted. I thought clarity would save relationships.

It didn’t.

Peace is when you stop bleeding just to be understood.

Choosing peace is not dramatic. It’s quiet. It’s leaving without announcements.

It’s deciding that your nervous system matters more than familiarity.

This isn’t growth that looks good online. This is growth that lets me sleep.

What I chose instead

  • Boundaries with no guilt.
  • Mornings that don’t start with panic.
  • A version of me that doesn’t beg to be kept.
